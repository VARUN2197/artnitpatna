ART NIT PATNA

We love ART!

Art Nit Patna is an online platform for the students of Nit patna to showcase their artistic talents. Students can submit their entries through the submit tab and our website will pubish them online. Any form of art is welcome. Do you love doodling? Are you fond of Photoshop or picsArt editing ? Or are you just the same old painter? If you find yourself among any of these, here's a nice oppourtunity. Explore the world of art in our comunity!
